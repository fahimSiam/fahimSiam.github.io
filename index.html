THESIS: CORRELATING LOCKDOWNS, MORTALITY RATES, AND AIR POLLUTION: A DEEP LEARNING IMBUED STUDY OF COVID-19

We determined the effects COVID-19lockdowns had on the air quality of different cities and found a proportionate relation between air quality and COVID deaths.

This is my thesis topic where we analazed the impact COVID-19 lockdowns has left on the air quality of different cities in the world using Python. We made alot of digrams finding relations between before lockdown and after lockdown air quality massurements visualizing the impact of the lockdowns. We showed the impact COVID had using clusters of cities in europe.

We also used alot of co-founding factors and came to a conclusion that the an element of air quality PM2.5 has a direct relation with COVID deaths using data from all USA counties.
